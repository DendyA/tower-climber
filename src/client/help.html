Welcome to Tower Climber!

How To Play
  • To begin playing Tower Climber, join a room from the main page.
  • Once you have joined a room, you will need to wait until the room has reached capacity.
  • As soon as the room has reached capacity, a countdown will begin.
  • You are free to move around during the countdown, but at 3 seconds remaining, you will be reset to your starting position!
  • When the clock reaches 0, race your friends to the top of the map.
  • First player to the top wins!!
Controls
  • Left and Right arrows to move laterally
  • Space Bar to jump
